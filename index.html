About Derge mylz Hospital

A modern community hospital in Dar es Salaam, focused on safe, affordable care.

For every patient, we combine compassionate care with evidence‑based medicine. Our team of doctors, nurses, and specialists work together so that you feel heard, respected, and supported at every step of your treatment.
Key highlights

• 24/7 emergency department
• Outpatient and specialist clinics
• In‑house laboratory and pharmacy
• Safe maternity and newborn care

Our Services & Departments

Comprehensive medical services under one roof, so your family can stay close to home.

Outpatient Clinic
General consultations, follow‑ups, and chronic disease care with experienced clinicians.
Emergency 24/7
Immediate care for accidents, sudden illnesses, and urgent medical conditions.
Maternity & Newborn
Antenatal care, safe delivery, and postnatal support for mothers and babies.
Laboratory Services
Routine and advanced tests to support fast, accurate diagnosis and treatment.
Radiology & Imaging
Ultrasound and other imaging services to guide clinical decisions.
Pharmacy
Quality, approved medicines with clear counselling on how to use them.
